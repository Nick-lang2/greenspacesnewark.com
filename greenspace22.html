Discover the Beauty of Newark's Green Spaces

Newark, NJ is home to a variety of parks and green areas that provide spaces for recreation, relaxation, and connection with nature.

Branch Brook Park

Famous for its cherry blossoms, Branch Brook Park is the oldest county park in the United States, offering over 360 acres of green space.

Weequahic Park

Home to Newark's largest lake, Weequahic Park is perfect for jogging, picnicking, and enjoying its natural beauty.

Military Park

Located in downtown Newark, Military Park is a vibrant space with public art, seasonal markets, and outdoor activities.

Riverfront Park

This park offers scenic views of the Passaic River, along with trails, sports fields, and community events.
